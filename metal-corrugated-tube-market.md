<p><p>Metal corrugated tubes are commonly categorized into two main types: single wall and double wall corrugated tubes. Single wall corrugated tubes are made up of a single layer of corrugated material, providing flexibility and economical solutions for various applications. On the other hand, double wall corrugated tubes consist of two layers of corrugated material, offering higher strength and durability. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and HVAC for fluid transportation, cable protection, and structural support applications.</p></p>
