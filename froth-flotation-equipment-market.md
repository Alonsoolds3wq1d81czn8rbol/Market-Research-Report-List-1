<p><p>Froth flotation equipment market includes various types such as Mechanical Agitating Flotation Machine, Self-Aspirated Flotation, Air Inflation Flotation, and Others. Mechanical Agitating Flotation Machines use mechanical agitation to mix and separate minerals. Self-Aspirated Flotation machines rely on air drawn into the impeller to create bubbles for mineral separation. Air Inflation Flotation machines introduce air through a mechanical or piston-driven method. Other types may include column flotation, dissolved air flotation, or hybrid systems combining different techniques for mineral separation.</p></p>
