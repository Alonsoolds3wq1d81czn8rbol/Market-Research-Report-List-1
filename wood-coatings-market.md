<p><ul><li>Oil-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood coatings are used to enhance and protect wooden surfaces from environmental factors like moisture, UV radiation, and fungi. There are three main types of wood coatings in the market: oil-based, water-based, and solvent-based. Oil-based coatings are made from natural oils and provide excellent durability and moisture resistance. Water-based coatings are made with water as the solvent and offer low VOC emissions, quick drying time, and easy application. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents and have high durability and resistance to chemicals, although they emit higher levels of VOCs.</p></p>
