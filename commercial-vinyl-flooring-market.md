<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vinyl flooring market consists of two main types: Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring and Homogeneous Sheet Flooring. Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring is made up of multiple layers with different materials, providing versatility in design options and better durability. On the other hand, Homogeneous Sheet Flooring is composed of one single layer throughout, making it easy to maintain and offering consistent color and pattern options. Both types are popular choices in commercial settings due to their durability, ease of maintenance, and a wide range of design options available.</p></p>
