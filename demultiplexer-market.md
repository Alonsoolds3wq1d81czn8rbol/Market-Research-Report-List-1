<p><p>Demultiplexer market is divided into three main types based on the technology used: BICMOS, bipolar, and CMOS. BICMOS demultiplexers combine the speed advantages of bipolar technology with the low-power benefits of CMOS. Bipolar demultiplexers offer high-speed performance and high output drive capability, making them suitable for applications requiring high-speed data transmission. CMOS demultiplexers are known for their low power consumption and high integration capability, making them ideal for battery-powered devices and portable electronics. Each type of demultiplexer has its own advantages and target market.</p></p>
