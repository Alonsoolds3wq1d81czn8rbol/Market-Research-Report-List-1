<p><strong>The Textile Surfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Cationic</li><li>Non-ionic</li><li>Amphoteric/Zwitterionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile surfactants are classified into four main types based on their chemical structure and charge: anionic, cationic, non-ionic, and amphoteric/zwitterionic. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge, cationic surfactants carry a positive charge, and non-ionic surfactants have no charge. Amphoteric/zwitterionic surfactants contain both positive and negative charges. Each type of surfactant offers unique properties and benefits for various textile applications, such as emulsification, wetting, foaming, and anti-static properties. These surfactants play a crucial role in the textile industry for manufacturing processes and product performance.</p></p>
