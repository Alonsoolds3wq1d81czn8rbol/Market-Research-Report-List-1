<p><p>The Aeronautical Satcom market includes various types such as L-Band, FSS Ku-Band, GEO-HTS Ku-Band, and GEO-HTS Ka-Band. GEO-HTS Ku-Band offers higher throughput and capacity for data-intensive applications. GEO-HTS Ka-Band provides even higher data rates and capacity for demanding aeronautical communication needs.
