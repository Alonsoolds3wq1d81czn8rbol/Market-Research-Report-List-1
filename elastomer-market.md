<p><p>Elastomers are polymers with elastic properties, mainly classified into three types: rubber, plastic, and silica gel. Rubber elastomers, such as natural rubber and synthetic rubber, are widely used in various applications due to their flexibility and resilience. Plastic elastomers, also known as thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), combine the properties of rubber and plastic, offering increased durability and versatility. Silica gel elastomers, made from silicon dioxide, provide superior moisture-absorbing capabilities and are commonly used in desiccants and sealants. These elastomers play a crucial role in diverse industries for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
