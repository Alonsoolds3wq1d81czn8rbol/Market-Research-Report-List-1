<p><p>Active Epoxy Toughening Agents are additives used to improve the toughness and impact resistance of epoxy resins. There are two main types of toughening agents: Rubber Toughening Agents and Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents. Rubber Toughening Agents consist of elastomers dispersed in the epoxy matrix to absorb energy and enhance flexibility. Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents involve adding thermoplastic particles to the epoxy to increase toughness and resistance to cracking. Both types of toughening agents help to improve the overall performance and durability of epoxy-based materials.</p></p>
