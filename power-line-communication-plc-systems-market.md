<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) Systems are divided into two market types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC systems operate at lower frequencies (up to 500 kHz) and are commonly used for simple applications such as smart meters and home automation. On the other hand, Broadband PLC systems operate at higher frequencies (above 1 MHz) and are used for more data-intensive applications like video streaming and high-speed internet access. Both market types cater to different communication needs and have their own set of advantages and limitations.</p></p>
