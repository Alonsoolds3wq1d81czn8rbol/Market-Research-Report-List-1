<p><p>Non-destructive testing equipment market includes various types of technologies used for inspecting and evaluating the integrity of materials and structures without causing damage. X-Ray testing involves using X-ray radiation to detect internal defects in an object. Ultrasonic testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws and measure thickness. Magnetic particle testing uses a magnetic field to identify surface defects. Penetrant flaw detection applies a liquid dye to identify surface defects. Eddy current testing employs electromagnetic induction to detect flaws. Other methods such as visual inspection and radiography are also used in this market.</p></p>
