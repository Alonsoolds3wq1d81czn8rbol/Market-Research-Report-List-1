<p><p>The automotive amplifier market can be divided into two main categories: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM amplifiers are installed by car manufacturers at the time of production, while aftermarket amplifiers are added by consumers or third-party installers after the vehicle is purchased. OEM amplifiers are typically tailored to the specific make and model of the vehicle, while aftermarket amplifiers offer more customization options and upgrades for enhanced sound quality and performance.</p></p>
