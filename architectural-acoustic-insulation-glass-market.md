<p><p>Architectural Acoustic Insulation Glass Market offers three main types of glass options: Hollow Glass, Vacuum Glass, and Laminated Glass. Hollow Glass features a hollow space between two glass panes to provide sound insulation. Vacuum Glass consists of two glass panes with a vacuum-sealed space in between, offering excellent soundproofing properties. Laminated Glass consists of two or more glass panes with an interlayer for enhanced sound insulation. Each type of glass offers unique benefits and options for architectural acoustic insulation solutions.</p></p>
