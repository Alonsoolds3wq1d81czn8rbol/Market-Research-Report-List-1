<p><p>Automotive optical sensors can be categorized into three main types: analog sensors, digital sensors, and other sensors. Analog sensors provide continuous output signals that vary based on the detected light intensity. Digital sensors convert light signals into digital format, providing precise and accurate measurements. Each sensor type plays a crucial role in enhancing safety, convenience, and efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
