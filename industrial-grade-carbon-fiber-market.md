<p><p>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber and Large-Tow Carbon Fiber are two types of industrial grade carbon fiber used in various industries. Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber is characterized by its smaller diameter fibers, typically around 5-10 microns, making it ideal for applications requiring high strength and stiffness. Large-Tow Carbon Fiber, on the other hand, features thicker fibers, typically around 20-50 microns, providing higher impact resistance and thermal conductivity. Both types of carbon fiber offer high-performance properties, but their specific characteristics make them suitable for different applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sports equipment.</p></p>
