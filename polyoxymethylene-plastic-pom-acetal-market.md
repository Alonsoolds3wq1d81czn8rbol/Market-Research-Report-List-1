Homopolymer POM is a material with high stiffness, strength, and hardness, while Copolymer POM offers improved thermal stability and chemical resistance. Both types of POM are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and consumer goods due to their excellent mechanical properties and versatility in applications.</p></p>
