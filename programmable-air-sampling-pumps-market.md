<p><p>Programmable air sampling pumps are commonly categorized into two types: high flow pumps and low flow pumps. High flow pumps are designed to sample larger volumes of air at a faster rate, making them suitable for monitoring contaminant levels in industrial settings or outdoor environments. In contrast, low flow pumps are tailored for sampling smaller amounts of air at a slower rate, making them ideal for indoor air quality testing or personal exposure monitoring. Both types offer programmable features for customized sampling protocols.</p></p>
