<p><ul><li>Independent Suspension</li><li>Non-independent Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle suspension system market is divided into two main types: Independent Suspension and Non-independent Suspension. Independent Suspension allows each wheel to move independently, providing a smoother ride and better handling on rough terrain. On the other hand, Non-independent Suspension connects both wheels on the same axle, resulting in a simpler and more cost-effective design. Both types of suspension systems have their own advantages and are used in different types of vehicles based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
