<p><ul><li>Medium Density Fibreboard</li><li>Hardboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed fibreboard market is divided into two main types: Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) and Hardboard. MDF is made by compressing wood fibers with resin, resulting in a dense and smooth board ideal for furniture and cabinetry. Hardboard, on the other hand, is made by compressing wood fibers without resin, creating a harder and more durable board suitable for flooring and construction projects. Both types of fibreboard offer versatility and strength, making them popular choices in the construction and furniture industries.</p></p>
