<p><p>Post and parcel sorting equipment market is categorized into different types based on their sorting mechanisms. The Single Split Tray Sorter uses trays that split in half to divert parcels into different directions. The Cross Tray Sorter utilizes trays that move in a cross-shaped pattern to sort parcels efficiently. The Single Push Tray Sorter uses trays that push parcels onto chutes for sorting. Other types of sorting equipment include tilt tray sorters, shoe sorters, and conveyor belt sorters. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific sorting needs of the operation.</p></p>
