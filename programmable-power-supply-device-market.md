<p><p>Programmable power supply devices are available in three main types: Single-output type, which provides one voltage output; Dual-output type, which offers two independent voltage outputs; and Multiple-output type, which can provide three or more voltage outputs simultaneously. Each type caters to different application needs and requirements, with single-output being suitable for simpler applications, dual-output for more complex setups requiring two different voltages, and multiple-output for applications needing multiple voltage sources. This variety in the market allows users to choose the right device for their specific power supply needs.</p></p>
