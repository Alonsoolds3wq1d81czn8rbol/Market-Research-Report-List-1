<p><p>Carpet back coating refers to the process of applying a layer of material to the back of a carpet to improve its performance and durability. The different types of carpet back coating include pre-coating, foam coating, textile back-coating, heavy coating, reinforcement, and back finish. Pre-coating involves applying a layer of adhesive to the back of the carpet before adding a secondary backing. Foam coating uses a foam layer for cushioning and support. Textile back-coating involves applying a fabric layer for added strength. Heavy coating uses a thicker layer for enhanced durability. Reinforcement involves adding additional materials for increased support. Back finish refers to the final layer applied for a smooth and protective finish.</p></p>
