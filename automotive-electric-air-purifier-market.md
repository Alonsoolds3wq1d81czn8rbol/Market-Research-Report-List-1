<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Air Purifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Electrical Charges Type</li><li>Positive Electrical Charges Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Negative Electrical Charges Type of automotive electric air purifiers emits negatively charged ions into the air, which attract and neutralize positively charged particles such as dust, pollen, and pet dander. This type of purifier is effective in removing airborne contaminants and improving air quality within the vehicle. On the other hand, the Positive Electrical Charges Type purifiers release positively charged ions to neutralize negatively charged particles. Both types work by creating a balance of charges in the air to improve overall air quality in the vehicle.</p></p>
