<p><p>The Hotel Smart Sensor Lock market has two main types: Magnetic Sensor Locks and Fingerprint Sensor Locks. Magnetic sensor locks use magnets to detect whether the door is open or closed, providing a simple and reliable security solution. Fingerprint sensor locks, on the other hand, use biometric technology to scan a person's fingerprint for access, offering a more advanced level of security. Both types of locks are commonly used in hotels to provide guests with secure and convenient entry to their rooms.</p></p>
