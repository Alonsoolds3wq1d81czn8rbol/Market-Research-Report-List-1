<p><strong>The RF Circulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coaxial Circulator</li><li>Drop-in Circulator</li><li>Surface Mount Circulator</li><li>Microstrip Circulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Circulators are devices used to direct the flow of signals in a specific direction within a circuit. There are four main types of RF Circulators: Coaxial Circulators, Drop-in Circulators, Surface Mount Circulators, and Microstrip Circulators. </p><p>Coaxial Circulators are housed in a cylindrical package and have three ports. Drop-in Circulators are designed to be mounted directly onto a circuit board. Surface Mount Circulators are smaller in size and can be directly soldered onto a circuit board. Microstrip Circulators are integrated into microstrip circuits for compact designs.</p></p>
