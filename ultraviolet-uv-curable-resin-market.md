<p><strong>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monomers</li><li>Oligomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) curable resins are categorized into three main types: monomers, oligomers, and photoinitiators. Monomers are the building blocks of the resin and provide reactivity to the formulation. Oligomers help to improve the mechanical properties and flexibility of the cured resin. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the curing process when exposed to UV light. Each of these components plays a vital role in the curing process of UV curable resins, contributing to the overall performance and characteristics of the final product.</p></p>
