<p><p>Acrylic Polymers Pressure Sensitive Adhesives are available in three main types - Solvent Type, Emulsion Type, and Light Curing Type. Solvent Type adhesives are based on a solvent that evaporates after application. Emulsion Type adhesives are water-based and easily washable. Light Curing Type adhesives require exposure to UV or visible light for curing. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications depending on factors like bonding strength, drying time, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
