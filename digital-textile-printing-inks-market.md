<p><p>The Digital Textile Printing Inks Market is segmented into four main types: Dispersed inks, Reactive inks, Acid inks, and Pigment inks. Dispersed inks are known for their high color intensity and are ideal for synthetic fabrics. Reactive inks are designed for natural fibers like cotton and linen, providing vibrant and long-lasting prints. Acid inks are mainly used for protein fibers like silk and wool. Pigment inks offer excellent color fastness and are suitable for a wide range of fabrics. Each type caters to specific textile printing requirements.</p></p>
