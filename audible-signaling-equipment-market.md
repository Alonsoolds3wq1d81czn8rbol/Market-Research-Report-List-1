<p><p>Audible Signaling Equipment Market consists of two main types: Wired Control and Wireless Control. Wired Control systems use physical cables to transmit signals between the control panel and the signaling devices, making them more stable and less susceptible to interference. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications based on the specific needs of the users.</p></p>
