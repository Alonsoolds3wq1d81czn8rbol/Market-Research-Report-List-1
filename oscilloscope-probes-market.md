<p><p>Oscilloscope Probes market consists of three main types: Passive Probes, Active Probes, and other specialized probes. Other specialized probes include differential probes for measuring voltage differences and current probes for measuring electrical currents. Each type serves different functions and caters to varying testing needs, providing users with a wide range of options for accurate and reliable signal measurements.</p></p>
