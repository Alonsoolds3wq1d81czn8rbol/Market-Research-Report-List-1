<p><p>Tankless water purifiers are becoming increasingly popular, with two main types dominating the market - RO water purifiers and UV water purifiers. RO water purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants from water, while UV water purifiers use ultraviolet light to disinfect water and kill bacteria and viruses. Both types are efficient, environmentally friendly, and provide clean and safe drinking water for households. The choice between RO and UV water purifiers depends on the specific water quality and contaminant concerns of each household.</p></p>
