<p><p>The Vehicle 3D LiDAR Sensor market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. </p><p>Mechanical LiDAR sensors use spinning mirrors to create a 3D map of the environment. They have a higher resolution but are slower and less durable. </p><p>On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR sensors use fixed components like micro-electromechanical mirrors to scan the surroundings. They are faster and more robust than mechanical LiDAR sensors, but currently have lower resolution. Both types of sensors are crucial for enabling autonomous driving technology.</p></p>
