<p><p>3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment in PCB market offers two main types - Inline 3D AOI and Offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated into the PCB assembly line, allowing for real-time inspection and immediate feedback for quick adjustments. Offline 3D AOI, on the other hand, operates separately from the production line, providing a more thorough and detailed inspection of PCBs. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of PCB assemblies.</p></p>
