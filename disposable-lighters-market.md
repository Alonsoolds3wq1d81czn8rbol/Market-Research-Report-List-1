<p><strong>The Disposable Lighters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flint Lighters</li><li>Electronic Lighters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable lighters are divided into two main market types: Flint lighters and electronic lighters. Flint lighters rely on a small metal wheel that rubs against a flint to produce a spark, igniting the gas from the fuel chamber. These lighters are commonly referred to as traditional lighters. Electronic lighters, on the other hand, use a battery-powered heating element to ignite the fuel, eliminating the need for flints and sparks. They are known for their convenience and reliability. Both types of lighters cater to different preferences and needs in the lighter market.</p></p>
