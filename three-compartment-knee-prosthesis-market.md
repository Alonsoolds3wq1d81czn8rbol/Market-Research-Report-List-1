<p><strong>The Three-compartment Knee Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cemented Bone</li><li>Non-Cemented Bone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cemented bone knee prostheses involve using bone cement to attach the prosthesis to the existing bone, offering stability and immediate weight-bearing. These prostheses are suitable for older patients with weaker bone structure. Non-cemented bone knee prostheses rely on natural bone growth to integrate with the prosthesis, providing long-term stability and reducing the risk of loosening over time. These prostheses are ideal for younger, more active patients with stronger bone quality. Both market types have their unique benefits and considerations for orthopedic surgeons and patients.</p></p>
