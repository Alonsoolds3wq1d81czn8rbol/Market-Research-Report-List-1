<p><p>The waterproof laminate flooring market is divided into two main types: Three Layer and Multilayer. Three Layer laminate flooring consists of three layers - a core layer, a design layer, and a protective layer. This type is more affordable and easy to install. Multilayer laminate flooring, on the other hand, has multiple layers that provide greater durability and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for areas prone to water exposure. Multilayer flooring is typically more expensive but offers superior performance and longevity.</p></p>
