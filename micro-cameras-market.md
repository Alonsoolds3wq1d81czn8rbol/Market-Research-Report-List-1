<p><p>Micro Cameras are widely used in various industries, with two key market types being NIR (Near-Infrared) Cameras and SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared) Cameras. NIR Cameras are sensitive to light in the near-infrared spectrum, allowing for applications such as night vision, surveillance, and medical imaging. On the other hand, SWIR Cameras detect light in the short-wave infrared spectrum, enabling applications like moisture detection, solar cell inspection, and semiconductor monitoring. Both types of cameras offer unique capabilities and are essential tools in today's technological landscape.</p></p>
