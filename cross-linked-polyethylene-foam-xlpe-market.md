<p><p>Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam (XLPE) is a versatile material used in various industries due to its properties of insulation, cushioning, and durability. It comes in two main types: physically cross-linked polyethylene foam, which is created through a process of heat and pressure, and chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam, which is formed by using chemical agents. Physically cross-linked foam tends to be more flexible and lightweight, while chemically cross-linked foam offers higher strength and heat resistance. Both types cater to different applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
