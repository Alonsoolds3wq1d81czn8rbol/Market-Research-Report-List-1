<p><strong>The Military Navigation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Devices</li><li>GNSS Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Navigation Device market is divided into two main types: GPS Devices and GNSS Devices. GPS Devices rely on signals from the Global Positioning System to determine location and provide accurate navigation information. On the other hand, GNSS Devices are more advanced and utilize signals from multiple global navigation satellite systems, such as GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, to enhance accuracy and reliability in challenging environments. Both types of devices play a crucial role in guiding military personnel and equipment during missions.</p></p>
