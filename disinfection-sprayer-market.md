<p><ul><li>Electrostatic</li><li>Non-Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disinfection sprayers can be classified into two main types: electrostatic and non-electrostatic. Electrostatic sprayers use electrical charges to create a wrap-around effect, ensuring even coverage of disinfectants on surfaces. They are efficient and effective in killing germs and viruses. On the other hand, non-electrostatic sprayers do not use electrical charges but still provide effective disinfection by delivering a fine mist of disinfectant. Both types of sprayers are essential tools in maintaining cleanliness and preventing the spread of illnesses.</p></p>
