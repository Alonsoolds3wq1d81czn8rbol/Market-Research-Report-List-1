<p><p>Stand-alone solar photovoltaic power generation systems are designed to operate independently and are not connected to the main electricity grid. Grid-connected solar photovoltaic power generation systems are connected to the main electricity grid and can export excess power back to the grid. Distributed solar photovoltaic power generation systems are smaller-scale systems that are integrated into buildings or local electricity networks, providing power at the point of consumption. These systems offer flexibility and reliability in generating renewable energy for various needs.</p></p>
