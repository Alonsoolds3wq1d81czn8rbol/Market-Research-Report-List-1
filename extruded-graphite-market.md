<p><p>Extruded Graphite Market includes various types such as Pitch Impregnated Type, Un-impregnated Type, Round Type, and Square Type. Pitch Impregnated Type graphite is infused with a pitch-based binder for increased strength and thermal conductivity. Un-impregnated Type graphite lacks any added binder, making it more porous and lightweight. Round Type graphite is shaped into cylindrical rods, while Square Type graphite is molded into square-shaped blocks. Each type offers unique properties and is used in different applications within industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
