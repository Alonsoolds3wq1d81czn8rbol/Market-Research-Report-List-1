<p><strong>The Textile Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile Inks are used for printing on fabrics and come in different types. Reactive Dye Inks are known for their excellent color fastness and ability to bond with fabric fibers. Acidic Inks are used for printing on protein-based fibers like silk and wool. Paint Inks are thick and have good coverage on fabrics. Dispersion Inks are water-based and suitable for digital textile printing. Sublimation Inks are used for printing on synthetic fabrics and produce vibrant colors through a heat transfer process.</p></p>
