<p><p>Silicone surgical cannulae are medical devices commonly used in plastic surgery procedures. The two main types in the market are curved cannulae and straight cannulae. Curved cannulae are designed with a gentle curve at the end, making them ideal for accessing hard-to-reach areas and creating precise incisions. Straight cannulae, on the other hand, are straight in shape and are often used for more direct and controlled movements during surgery. Both types offer surgeons flexibility and precision in their surgical procedures.</p></p>
