<p><p>All-Terrain Tires are divided into two main types in the market - Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires have plies layered diagonally across the tire, providing a sturdy and durable construction suitable for rough terrains. Radial tires have plies that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better traction and a smoother ride on various terrains. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices among off-road enthusiasts and drivers looking for reliable performance in challenging conditions.</p></p>
