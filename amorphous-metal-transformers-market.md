<p><strong>The Amorphous Metal Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Amorphous Metal Transformers</li><li>Dry-Type Amorphous Metal Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous Metal Transformers come in two main types: Oil-Immersed and Dry-Type transformers. Oil-Immersed Amorphous Metal Transformers are filled with oil for cooling and insulation, making them suitable for high-power applications. Dry-Type Amorphous Metal Transformers, on the other hand, use air or resin for cooling and do not require oil, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to maintain. Both types offer improved energy efficiency and reduced losses compared to traditional transformers, making them a popular choice for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
