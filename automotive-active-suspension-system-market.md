<p><p>Automotive active suspension systems utilize advanced technology to provide better ride comfort, handling, and stability. There are four main types of active suspension systems: Air suspension systems use compressed air to adjust ride height and stiffness. Hydraulic suspension systems use hydraulic fluid for similar adjustments. Electromagnetic suspension systems use electromagnetic technology to control suspension movement. Electro-hydraulic suspension systems combine hydraulic fluid and electronic controls for precise adjustments. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of performance and customization options for different driving conditions.</p></p>
