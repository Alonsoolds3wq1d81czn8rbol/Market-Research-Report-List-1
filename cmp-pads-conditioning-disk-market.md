<p><p>Conventional CMP Pads Conditioners are used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing silicon wafers. These conditioners typically use abrasive particles bonded onto a disk to remove debris and contaminants from the polishing pad surface. On the other hand, CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners utilize diamond-coated disks for a more efficient and consistent conditioning process. The diamond-coated disks offer superior durability and performance, making them ideal for high-precision CMP applications. Both types of conditioners play a crucial role in maintaining the effectiveness and longevity of CMP pads in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
