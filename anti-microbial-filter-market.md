<p><p>The Anti-Microbial Filter market includes various types such as Non-Woven Fiber Filter, Nano Fiber Filter, and Porous Polymeric Filter. Non-Woven Fiber Filters are made of tangled fibers that trap particles through mechanical filtration. Nano Fiber Filters have ultrafine fibers that capture bacteria and viruses at the nanoscale level. Porous Polymeric Filters have a network of pores that physically block microbes from passing through. These filters are designed to effectively remove harmful microorganisms and improve indoor air quality.</p></p>
