<p><p>The SOC chip market is categorized into three types based on the complexity of the chips being produced. MSI (Medium-Scale Integration) chips contain a moderate number of transistors and components. LSI (Large-Scale Integration) chips are more complex, containing a larger number of transistors and components than MSI chips. VLSI (Very Large-Scale Integration) chips are the most complex, with an extremely high number of transistors and components packed into a single chip. Each type of market caters to different applications and requirements of electronic devices.</p></p>
