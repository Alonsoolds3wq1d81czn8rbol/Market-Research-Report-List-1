<p><p>Denture adhesives are used in both direct restoration bonding and indirect restoration bonding markets. Direct restoration bonding involves securing the dentures directly to the patient's teeth using adhesive materials. Indirect restoration bonding, on the other hand, involves bonding the dentures to abutment teeth or implant abutments. Both types of bonding are essential in ensuring the stability and longevity of dentures for patients. Denture adhesives play a crucial role in maintaining a secure and comfortable fit for dentures in both direct and indirect restoration bonding scenarios.</p></p>
