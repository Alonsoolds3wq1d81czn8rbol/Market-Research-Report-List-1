<p><ul><li>Polytetrafluoroethylene</li><li>Polyethylene</li><li>Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powdered wax markets can be classified into three main types: Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyethylene, and Polypropylene. PTFE powdered wax is known for its excellent non-stick and low friction properties, making it ideal for applications in cookware and industrial coatings. Polyethylene powdered wax is commonly used in various industries due to its good abrasion resistance and lubrication properties. Polypropylene powdered wax, on the other hand, is known for its high melting point and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications in the automotive and packaging industries.</p></p>
