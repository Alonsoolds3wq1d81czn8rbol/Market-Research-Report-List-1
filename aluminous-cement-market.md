<p><p>Aluminous cement, also known as high-alumina cement, is a type of cement which is made by fusing a mixture of limestone and bauxite in a kiln at high temperatures. This results in a clinker that contains a high percentage of alumina, typically around 50-70%. Aluminous cement is known for its high early strength and rapid setting time, making it ideal for applications where quick setting and high strength are required.</p><p>The Aluminous Cement Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to the increasing demand for high-performance construction materials in various industries such as construction, oil & gas, and mining. Moreover, the rising focus on sustainable and energy-efficient construction materials is further boosting the market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the aluminous cement market include the development of new formulations with enhanced properties such as improved durability, resistance to chemical attack, and reduced carbon footprint.
