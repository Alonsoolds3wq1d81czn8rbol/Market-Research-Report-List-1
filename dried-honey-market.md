<p><p>Dried honey is available in two main market types: granular and powder. Granular dried honey is made by dehydrating liquid honey into small, coarse granules, which can be used as a topping or mixed into foods and drinks. Powdered dried honey is created by further processing the granules into a fine powder texture, which can easily dissolve in liquids or be used in baked goods and cooking. Both types offer a convenient and versatile way to enjoy the natural sweetness of honey in various forms.</p></p>
