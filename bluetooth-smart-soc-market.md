<p><p>Bluetooth Smart SoC market can be divided into two main types: Bluetooth Smart and Bluetooth Smart Ready. Bluetooth Smart devices are low-energy devices that can connect to smartphones and other devices, allowing for data transfer and communication. On the other hand, Bluetooth Smart Ready devices are capable of connecting to both Bluetooth Smart devices and traditional Bluetooth devices, making them more versatile and able to support a wider range of applications. Both types of markets cater to different needs and use cases in the growing Internet of Things ecosystem.</p></p>
