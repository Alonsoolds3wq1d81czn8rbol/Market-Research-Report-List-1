<p><p>Amorphous Alloy Core Transformers are available in two main types: Oil-Immersed and Dry-Type. Oil-Immersed transformers are filled with insulating oil, which helps to dissipate heat and protect the transformer from moisture. These transformers are commonly used in outdoor applications. On the other hand, Dry-Type transformers use air or solid insulation to protect the core and windings. They are maintenance-free and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for indoor installations where flammable materials are a concern.</p></p>
