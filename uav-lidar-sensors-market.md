<p><p>UAV Lidar sensors come in two main types: Solid State Lidar Sensors and Mechanical Lidar Sensors. Solid State Lidar Sensors are compact and more durable as they have no moving parts, making them ideal for UAV applications requiring higher reliability and precision. Mechanical Lidar Sensors, on the other hand, use rotating mirrors to scan the environment, providing a wider field of view. Both types have their strengths and are suitable for different UAV applications based on factors such as accuracy, range, and cost.</p></p>
