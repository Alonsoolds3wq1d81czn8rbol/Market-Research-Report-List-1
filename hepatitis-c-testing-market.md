<p><p>Hepatitis C testing market consists of three main types: virus testing, antibody testing, and antigen testing. Virus testing involves detecting the presence of hepatitis C virus in a person's blood sample. Antibody testing identifies antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus, indicating a past or current infection. Antigen testing, on the other hand, detects specific viral proteins in the blood, indicating an active infection. These different testing methods help healthcare professionals diagnose and monitor hepatitis C infections in individuals.</p></p>
