<p><p>Polyacrylamide market types include non-ionic polyacrylamide (PAMN), anionic polyacrylamide (APAM), cationic polyacrylamide (CPAM), and others. Non-ionic polyacrylamide has high molecular weight and is used in water treatment. Anionic polyacrylamide is negatively charged and mainly used in wastewater treatment. Cationic polyacrylamide is positively charged and used in industries like papermaking and oil drilling. Other types of polyacrylamide cater to specific industrial needs such as mining and agriculture. Each type offers unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
