<p><strong>The Cast Resin Dry-Type Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concentric Type</li><li>Overlap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast resin dry-type transformers are available in two main types: concentric type and overlap type. Concentric type transformers feature coils that are wound around the core in a single layer, creating a more efficient design with reduced losses. On the other hand, overlap type transformers have coils that are wound around the core in multiple layers, providing better insulation and cooling properties. Both types offer high reliability, efficiency, and safety, making them popular choices for various applications in the electrical industry.</p></p>
