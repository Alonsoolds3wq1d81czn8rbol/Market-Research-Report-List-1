<p><p>The Automobile Wheel Hub Motor market consists of two main types: Inner-Rotor Motor and External-Rotor Motor. Inner-Rotor Motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, resulting in a compact design ideal for smaller vehicles. External-Rotor Motors, on the other hand, have the rotor placed outside the stator, allowing for greater torque and power output, making them suitable for larger vehicles such as trucks and SUVs. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different vehicle sizes and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
