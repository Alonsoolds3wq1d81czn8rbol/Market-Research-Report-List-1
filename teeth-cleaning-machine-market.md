<p><p>The teeth cleaning machine market consists of two main types: tooth scalers and tooth polishers. Tooth scalers are used to remove plaque and tartar buildup from the teeth, especially in hard-to-reach areas. They typically use vibration or ultrasonic technology to dislodge the plaque. On the other hand, tooth polishers are designed to remove stains from the teeth and give them a smooth and polished appearance. These machines usually employ a rotating polishing cup or a rubber cup with polishing paste to achieve the desired results.</p></p>
