<p><p>Ceramic textile market types can be broadly categorized into three main categories: refractory ceramic fiber (RCF), low bio-persistent ceramic fibers, and polycrystalline ceramic fibers. RCFs are known for their high temperature resistance and thermal insulation properties. Low bio-persistent ceramic fibers are designed to reduce health risks associated with exposure. Polycrystalline ceramic fibers are characterized by their high strength and durability. Each type serves specific industrial applications where high temperature resistance and thermal insulation are crucial.</p></p>
