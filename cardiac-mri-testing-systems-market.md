<p><p>Cardiac MRI testing systems refer to medical equipment used to diagnose heart conditions through magnetic resonance imaging. There are two main types of MRI systems used for cardiac testing: open MRI systems and closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems have a more spacious design, making them suitable for claustrophobic patients or those who require additional space during the test. Closed MRI systems have a more compact design and are generally more powerful, providing a higher resolution image of the heart for more accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
