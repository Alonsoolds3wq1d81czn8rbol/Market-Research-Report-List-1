<p><strong>The Front and Rear Air-Conditioning (AC) Thermal Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop System</li><li>Closed Loop System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front and Rear Air-Conditioning (AC) Thermal Systems are commonly categorized into two types based on the circulation of refrigerant: Open Loop System and Closed Loop System. In an Open Loop System, the refrigerant constantly circulates between the front and rear units without any barrier, while a Closed Loop System uses a barrier to separate the refrigerant flow between the front and rear units. Each system has its advantages and drawbacks, and the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the vehicle and desired performance.</p></p>
