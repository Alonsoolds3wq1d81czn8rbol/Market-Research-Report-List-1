<p><p>Semiconductor water filters are categorized into different types based on their material composition. Nylon filters are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals. PTFE filters offer superior chemical resistance and are excellent for high-temperature applications. Activated carbon filters are effective in removing impurities and odors from water. Meltblown polypro filters are commonly used for fine particle filtration. Other types of semiconductor water filters include ceramic, cellulose, and polyester filters, each offering unique features and benefits for water purification.</p></p>
