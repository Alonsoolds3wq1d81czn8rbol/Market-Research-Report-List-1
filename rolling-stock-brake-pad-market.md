<p><p>Rolling stock brake pads are used in trains and other transportation vehicles to provide braking performance. There are two main types of brake pads in the market - composite brake pads and powder metallurgy brake pads. Composite brake pads are made from a mixture of materials such as rubber, Kevlar, and fiberglass, offering good performance and durability. Powder metallurgy brake pads are made from metal powders compressed and sintered together, providing high friction and heat resistance. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the rolling stock industry.</p></p>
