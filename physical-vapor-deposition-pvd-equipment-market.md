<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Equipment market consists of two main types: Sputtering Deposition and Evaporation Deposition. Sputtering Deposition involves bombarding a target material with high-energy ions to create a thin film on a substrate, while Evaporation Deposition involves heating a material in a vacuum to create a vapor that condenses on a substrate. Both methods are widely used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace for coating surfaces with thin films for various applications.</p></p>
