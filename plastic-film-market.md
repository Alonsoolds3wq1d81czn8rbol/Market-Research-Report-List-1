<p><p>Plastic film market is divided into three main types: PE, BOPP, and BOPET. PE (polyethylene) film is versatile and commonly used for packaging and household products. BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) film is known for its clarity, strength, and moisture barrier properties, making it ideal for packaging snacks, confectionery, and tobacco products. BOPET (biaxially oriented polyester) film is a high-performance material with excellent strength, clarity, and temperature resistance, commonly used for flexible packaging and industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs in the market.</p></p>
