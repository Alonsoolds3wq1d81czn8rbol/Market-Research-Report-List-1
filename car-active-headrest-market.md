<p><ul><li>Headrest Activation</li><li>Seat Back Activation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car Active Headrest can be categorized into two main types based on the method of activation: Headrest Activation and Seat Back Activation. Headrest Activation involves sensors in the headrest that detect a rear-end collision and automatically move the headrest forward to reduce the risk of whiplash. Seat Back Activation, on the other hand, requires the entire back of the seat to move forward in response to a collision. Both types are designed to improve passenger safety and reduce the likelihood of neck and head injuries during accidents.</p></p>
