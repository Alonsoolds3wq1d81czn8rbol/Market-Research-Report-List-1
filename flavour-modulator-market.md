<p><p>Flavour modulators are ingredients that can enhance, suppress, or modify different taste sensations in food and beverages. The four main types of flavour modulators are sweet modulators, which enhance sweetness; salt modulators, which enhance or reduce saltiness; mouthfeel modulators, which alter the texture and sensation of food in the mouth; and masking modulators, which mitigate unwanted flavors or bitterness. These modulators are used by food manufacturers to create unique and balanced taste profiles in their products.</p></p>
