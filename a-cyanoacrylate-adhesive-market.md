<p><p>α-Cyanoacrylate adhesive market types include water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, reactive, and others. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives offer fast curing times and strong bonds. Hot melt adhesives are heated and applied in a liquid state, then solidify upon cooling. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to cure and form a strong bond. Other types of α-Cyanoacrylate adhesives include UV-curable and pressure-sensitive adhesives, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
