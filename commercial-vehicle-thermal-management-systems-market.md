<p><p>Commercial vehicle thermal management systems market consists of various types such as thermal management modules, electric fans, electric water pumps, radiators, and thermostats. Thermal management modules are used for controlling the temperature of the vehicle's engine and cabin. Electric fans help in cooling the radiator by blowing air over it. Electric water pumps circulate coolant throughout the engine to maintain optimal temperature. Radiators dissipate heat from the engine. Thermostats regulate the flow of coolant to keep the engine at the right temperature.</p></p>
