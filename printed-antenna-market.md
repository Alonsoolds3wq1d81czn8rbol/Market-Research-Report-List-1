<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Printed Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ink-jet Printing</li><li>Screen Printing</li><li>Flexography Printing</li><li>Gravure Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed antenna market includes various types of printing technologies such as ink-jet printing, screen printing, flexography printing, and gravure printing. Ink-jet printing involves spraying ink onto a substrate to create the antenna design. Screen printing uses a mesh stencil to transfer ink onto the substrate. Flexography printing utilizes flexible relief plates to print on various surfaces. Gravure printing involves etching the antenna design onto a cylinder and transferring ink onto the substrate. Each printing method offers unique advantages in terms of cost, quality, and flexibility.</p></p>
