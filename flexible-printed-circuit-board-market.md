<p><ul><li>Single-Sided FPCBs</li><li>Double-Sided FPCBs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-Sided FPCBs are flexible printed circuit boards that consist of a single layer of conductive material, ideal for simpler electronic devices. Double-Sided FPCBs have conductive material on both sides, allowing for more complex circuits and designs. Both types offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for applications where traditional rigid PCBs may not be practical. Single-Sided FPCBs are more cost-effective and easier to manufacture, while Double-Sided FPCBs provide increased design flexibility and functionality.</p></p>
