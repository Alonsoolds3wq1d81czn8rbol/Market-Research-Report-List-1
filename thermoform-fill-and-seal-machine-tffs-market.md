<p><p>Thermoform, Fill and Seal Machine (TFFS) market includes Fully Automatic and Semi-Automatic types. Fully Automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they carry out the entire process from forming the packaging material to filling and sealing. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic machines require manual loading of products but automate the rest of the process. Both types offer efficiency and convenience in packaging operations, with Fully Automatic machines being more suitable for high-volume production and Semi-Automatic machines being more cost-effective for smaller scale operations.</p></p>
