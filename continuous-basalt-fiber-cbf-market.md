<p><strong>The Continuous Basalt Fiber (CBF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal-Blowing</li><li>Centrifugal-Multiroll</li><li>Die-Blowing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Basalt Fiber (CBF) Market Types include Centrifugal-Blowing, Centrifugal-Multiroll, Die-Blowing. </p><p>Centrifugal-Blowing method involves melting basalt rocks and then blowing the molten material through nozzles to create fibers. Centrifugal-Multiroll method uses multiple rollers to spin and stretch the fibers before cooling them. Die-Blowing method involves extruding molten basalt material through a die to form fibers.</p><p>Each method offers different benefits in terms of fiber quality, production efficiency, and cost effectiveness, catering to various industries' specific needs.</p></p>
