<p><p>Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads are categorized into two main types - Thermal Inkjet Heads and Piezo Thin Film Inkjet Heads.</p><p>The Thermal Inkjet Heads work by heating the ink, causing it to expand and be ejected through a nozzle onto the printing surface. They are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and simplicity.</p><p>Piezo Thin Film Inkjet Heads utilize piezoelectric materials to generate pressure pulses, ejecting the ink droplets onto the printing surface. They are known for their high precision and ability to work with a wide range of ink types.</p></p>
