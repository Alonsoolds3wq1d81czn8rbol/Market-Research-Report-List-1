<p><p>In the automotive industry, the Smart Start Key market can be divided into two main types - 1 way and 2 way. A 1-way system allows the key fob to send signals to the vehicle, such as unlocking doors or starting the engine. On the other hand, a 2-way system not only sends signals to the vehicle but also receives feedback, providing the user with information on the status of the vehicle, like whether the doors are locked or the engine is running.</p></p>
