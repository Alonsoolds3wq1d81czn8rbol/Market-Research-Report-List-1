<p><p>Plasma thermal spray systems are utilized to deposit coatings on various surfaces for protection or enhancement. There are two main types of plasma systems: atmospheric plasma system and vacuum plasma system. Atmospheric plasma systems operate at atmospheric pressure and are suitable for larger-scale applications, while vacuum plasma systems work under reduced pressure conditions, offering higher purity and more precise control over the coating deposition process. Both types of systems are used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy for surface coating applications.</p></p>
