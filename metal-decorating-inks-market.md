<p><p>Metal decorating inks are used for printing on metal surfaces in various industries such as packaging and automotive. There are three main types of metal decorating inks on the market: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and easy to clean, while solvent-based inks offer good adhesion and durability. UV-cured inks are known for their fast drying time and high resistance to chemicals and abrasion. Each type of ink has its own unique benefits and applications in the metal decorating industry.</p></p>
