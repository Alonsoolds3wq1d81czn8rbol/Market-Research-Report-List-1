<p><ul><li>SMD LED Lamp Bead</li><li>Directly-inserted LED Lamp Beads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED lamp beads market can be divided into two main types: SMD LED lamp bead and directly-inserted LED lamp bead. </p><p>SMD LED lamp beads are surface-mount devices that are soldered onto the circuit board, providing high brightness and energy efficiency. </p><p>Directly-inserted LED lamp beads are directly inserted into the circuit board, offering ease of installation and high stability. </p><p>Both types of LED lamp beads cater to different market demands, with SMD LED lamp beads being more commonly used in commercial lighting applications and directly-inserted LED lamp beads favored for their simplicity and reliability in various electronic devices.</p></p>
