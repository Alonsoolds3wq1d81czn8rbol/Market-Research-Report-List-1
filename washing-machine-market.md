<p><p>The washing machine market can be categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and dryers. Fully automatic washing machines are equipped with advanced features and perform all functions automatically, from washing to drying. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention, as they need to be manually filled with water and the clothes need to be moved from the washing tub to the drying tub. Dryers are specifically designed for drying clothes and are often used in conjunction with washing machines to accelerate the drying process.</p></p>
