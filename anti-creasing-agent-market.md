<p><ul><li>Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li><li>Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dye-bath lubricant/anti-creasing agents are used in the dyeing process to prevent creases and wrinkles on fabrics, ensuring a smooth and even dye application. Wet processing lubricant/anti-creasing agents are utilized during various wet processing stages like washing and finishing to reduce friction between fibers and fabrics, minimizing creasing and ensuring a soft and smooth final product. Both types of anti-creasing agents play a crucial role in improving the quality and appearance of textiles during different stages of processing.</p></p>
