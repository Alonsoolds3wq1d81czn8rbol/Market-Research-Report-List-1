<p><p>Molecular sieve adsorbents are categorized into different types based on their pore sizes and structures. The most common types include 3A, 4A, 5A, Type X, and other specialized markets. The 3A molecular sieve is used for drying polar liquids, the 4A sieve is used for drying gases and liquids, while the 5A sieve is used for separating normal and isomerous alkane, absorbing moisture from unsaturated hydrocarbons, and the Type X sieve is used for separating oxygen and nitrogen. Other specialized markets cater to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
