<p><p>Powder tank trucks can be categorized into three main types based on the number of warehouses they have: Horizontal Single Warehouse, Horizontal Double Warehouse, and Horizontal Three Warehouses. In Horizontal Single Warehouse tanks, the powder is stored in a single compartment. In Horizontal Double Warehouse tanks, the powder is stored in two separate compartments. In Horizontal Three Warehouses tanks, the powder is stored in three compartments. Each type offers different storage options and capabilities based on the specific needs of the transportation and delivery of powdered materials.</p></p>
