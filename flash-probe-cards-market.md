<p><ul><li>MEMS Probe Cards</li><li>Non-MEMS Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS probe cards utilize microelectromechanical systems technology to create smaller, more precise probe tips for testing semiconductor devices. These types of probe cards are known for their higher accuracy and reliability, making them suitable for testing advanced technologies such as 5G and AI chips. Non-MEMS probe cards, on the other hand, use traditional probe tip manufacturing methods and are generally considered less advanced compared to MEMS probe cards. These probe cards are typically used for testing older semiconductor technologies or less complex devices.</p></p>
