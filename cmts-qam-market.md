<p><p>CMTS, or Cable Modem Termination System, serves as the key component in delivering high-speed internet access to households. There are two main market types: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). I-CMTS combines the CMTS core and downstream RF receiver in one unit, offering easier installation and management. On the other hand, M-CMTS separates these components, allowing for more scalability and flexibility in network design. Both types cater to different requirements of cable operators seeking efficient broadband solutions.</p></p>
