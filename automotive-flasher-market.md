<p><strong>The Automotive Flasher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Flasher</li><li>Thermal Flasher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive flasher market consists of two main types: electronic flasher and thermal flasher. Electronic flashers use solid-state circuitry to control the timing of the flashing lights, providing a more precise and reliable performance. On the other hand, thermal flashers rely on a bi-metallic strip to control the flashing timing, making them simpler in design and less expensive. Both types serve the purpose of controlling the flashing rate of turn signals, hazard lights, and other automotive indicators. Their choice depends on factors such as cost, longevity, and performance requirements.</p></p>
