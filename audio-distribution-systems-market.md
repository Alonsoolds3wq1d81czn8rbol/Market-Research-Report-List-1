<p><p>Audio distribution systems market can be classified into front systems and surround systems. Front systems typically consist of two speakers located in front of the listener to create a stereo sound experience. Surround systems, on the other hand, involve multiple speakers placed around the listener to immerse them in a 360-degree audio environment. Both types of systems cater to different preferences and purposes, with front systems usually suited for everyday listening and surround systems favored for cinematic or gaming experiences.</p></p>
