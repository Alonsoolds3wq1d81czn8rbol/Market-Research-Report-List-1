<p><p>Aviation actuator systems are essential components in aircraft controls. There are different types of actuator systems in the market including electric, electromechanical, electrohydraulic, and electrohydrostatic systems. </p><p>Electric actuator systems rely on electrical power to operate and control the movement of aircraft components. Electromechanical actuator systems combine electrical and mechanical systems for precise control. Electrohydraulic actuator systems use hydraulic power to move aircraft controls, while electrohydrostatic actuator systems utilize a combination of hydraulic and electrical power for enhanced performance. </p><p>Each type offers unique benefits and capabilities for aviation applications.</p></p>
