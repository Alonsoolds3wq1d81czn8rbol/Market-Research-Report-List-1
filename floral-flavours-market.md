<p><p>Floral flavours are categorized into three main types in the market: natural, organic, and artificial. Natural flavours are derived from real flowers through processes like steam distillation or solvent extraction. Organic flavours are produced from flowers grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. Artificial flavours are created in a laboratory to mimic the taste of flowers using synthetic chemicals. Consumers have the option to choose between these different types of floral flavours based on personal preferences and health concerns.</p></p>
