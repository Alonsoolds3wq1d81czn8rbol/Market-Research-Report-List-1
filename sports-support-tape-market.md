<p><p>Sports support tape is available in two main types: roll type and precut type. Roll type tape comes in a large roll that allows users to cut the tape to their desired length, making it more customizable for different body parts and sizes. On the other hand, precut type tape comes in pre-sized strips or shapes, providing convenience for quick application without the need for cutting. Both types offer varying levels of support and compression for athletes during physical activities.</p></p>
