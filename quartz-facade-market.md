<p><strong>The Quartz Facade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz Facade market consists of two main types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves shaping quartz slabs under high pressure to create precise designs. This method is commonly used for producing uniform facades with intricate patterns. On the other hand, Casting Molding involves pouring liquid quartz into molds and allowing it to harden, resulting in a more customizable and organic appearance. Both techniques offer unique advantages in terms of design flexibility and production efficiency in the Quartz Facade industry.</p></p>
