<p><p>A bimetal sensor is a type of sensor that consists of two different metals bonded together, which respond to temperature changes by bending due to the different thermal expansion rates of the metals. This bending is then converted into an electrical signal that can be used to measure temperature. Bimetal sensors are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, HVAC, and appliances for temperature monitoring and control.</p><p>The bimetal sensor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.70% during the forecast period.
