<p><p>End-of-line packaging machines are classified into automatic and semi-automatic types based on their level of automation. Automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, making them efficient for high-volume production environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input from operators, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations or when flexibility is needed in the packaging process. Both types of machines offer various benefits in terms of speed, precision, and labor savings, catering to different needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
