<p><p>Pharmaceutical sweeteners market can be classified into two main types: Nutritive sweeteners and Non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners provide calories and energy, such as sugar, honey, and maple syrup. On the other hand, non-nutritive sweeteners are low or zero in calories and are often used as sugar substitutes, like aspartame, sucralose, and stevia. Both types of sweeteners have a significant impact on the pharmaceutical industry, catering to consumers looking for alternatives to traditional sugar.</p></p>
