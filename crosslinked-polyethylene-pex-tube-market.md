<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PEX) Tube Market has three main types - PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced using the peroxide method, resulting in a high degree of crosslinking and superior properties such as flexibility and resistance to stress cracking. PE-Xb is manufactured through the silane method, offering a balance between flexibility and strength. PE-Xc is made using the electron beam method, providing enhanced thermal and mechanical properties. Each type caters to different applications based on specific requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
