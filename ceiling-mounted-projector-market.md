<p><ul><li>LCD Projector</li><li>DLP Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceiling mounted projectors come in two main types: LCD projectors and DLP projectors. LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to produce images on screen, resulting in vibrant colors and sharp details. DLP projectors, on the other hand, use digital micromirror devices to reflect light and create images, offering high contrast ratios and smooth motion. Both types are popular choices for home theaters, classrooms, and business presentations due to their versatility and advanced technology.</p></p>
