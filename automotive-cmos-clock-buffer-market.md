<p><p>Automotive CMOS Clock Buffer market consists of various types such as Differential Clock Buffers, Single Ended Clock Buffers, Zero Delay Buffer, and Fanout Buffers. Differential Clock Buffers provide improved signal integrity and noise immunity. Single Ended Clock Buffers are cost-effective and easy to implement. Zero Delay Buffers minimize clock skew issues. Fanout Buffers distribute clock signals to multiple output channels. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences in the automotive industry, ensuring reliable and efficient clock signal distribution in vehicles.</p></p>
