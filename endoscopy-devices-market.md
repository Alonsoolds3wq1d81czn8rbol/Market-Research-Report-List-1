<p><p>Endoscopy devices market is divided into two main types - flexible endoscopes and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are designed with a bendable insertion tube that allows for easier navigation through the body's various organs and pathways. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, have a straight and inflexible structure, making them suitable for more straightforward procedures. Both types of endoscopes play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating a wide range of medical conditions by providing clear and detailed visuals of internal structures.</p></p>
