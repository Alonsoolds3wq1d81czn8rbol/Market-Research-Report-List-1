<p><p>Thermochromic pigments are specialized pigments that change color in response to temperature fluctuations. There are two main types of thermochromic pigments: reversible and irreversible. Reversible thermochromic pigments change color temporarily and revert back to their original color when the temperature returns to normal. Irreversible thermochromic pigments change color permanently once they have been activated by heat. Both types have various applications in products such as clothing, toys, packaging, and security labels.</p></p>
