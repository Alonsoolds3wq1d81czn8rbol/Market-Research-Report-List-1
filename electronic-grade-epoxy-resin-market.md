<p><p>Electronic Grade Epoxy Resin is primarily divided into four types: Bromined Flame Retardant Epoxy Resin, Modified Phosphorous Epoxy Resin, Isocyanate Modified Epoxy Resin, and Other. Bromined Flame Retardant Epoxy Resin incorporates bromine compounds for enhanced fire resistance. Modified Phosphorous Epoxy Resin utilizes phosphorous compounds to achieve similar fire retardant properties. Isocyanate Modified Epoxy Resin combines isocyanate compounds for improved chemical resistance. The "Other" category encompasses various specialty epoxy resins with unique properties catering to specific electronic applications.</p></p>
