<p><ul><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Stent Type</li><li>Heat Sink Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Sprinklers Market Types include Glass Bubbles, Fusible Alloy, Stent Type, and Heat Sink Type. Glass Bubbles sprinklers use glass bulbs filled with liquid that bursts when exposed to heat. Fusible Alloy sprinklers have a metal alloy that melts at a specific temperature to release water. Stent Type sprinklers have a metal frame that collapses under heat to activate water flow. Heat Sink Type sprinklers use a heat sink mechanism to activate water release. Each type offers a unique method of fire suppression for different applications.</p></p>
