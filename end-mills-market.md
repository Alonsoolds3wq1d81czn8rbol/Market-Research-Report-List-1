<p><p>End mills are cutting tools used in milling applications to remove material from a workpiece. <p><ul><li>Peripheral Cutting Edge</li><li>End Cutting Edge</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The end mills market can be categorized into three main types: peripheral cutting edge, end cutting edge, and others. Peripheral cutting edge refers to end mills with cutting edges on the outside circumference of the tool, which are commonly used for side milling operations. End cutting edge end mills have cutting edges on the end face, allowing for plunge cutting and slotting. The "others" category includes a variety of end mills with unique designs and cutting edge configurations, catering to specific machining requirements.</p></p>
