<p><p>Functional composites are materials made up of two or more different components that have distinct physical and chemical properties. Metal Matrix Composites are a type of functional composite where a metal matrix is reinforced with a secondary phase material like ceramic or carbon fibers. On the other hand, Polymer Matrix Composites are made up of a polymer matrix filled with a reinforcement material like glass or carbon fibers. Both types of composites offer enhanced mechanical, thermal, and electrical properties compared to their individual components.</p></p>
