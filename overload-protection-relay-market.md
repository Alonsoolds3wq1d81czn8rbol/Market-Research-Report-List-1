<p><p>Overload protection relays are safety devices used to protect electrical systems from damage caused by excessive current flow. There are two main types of overload protection relays in the market: thermal overload relays and magnetic overload relays. Thermal overload relays operate by sensing the temperature rise caused by overcurrents, while magnetic overload relays use a magnetic mechanism to trip when current exceeds a certain threshold. Both types of relays are crucial in preventing electrical equipment from overheating and malfunctioning.</p></p>
