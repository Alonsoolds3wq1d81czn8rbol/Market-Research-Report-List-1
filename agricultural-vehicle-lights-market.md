Front lamps are designed to illuminate the path ahead and enhance visibility during nighttime or unfavorable weather conditions. Rear lamps, on the other hand, are used to enhance the visibility of the agricultural vehicle to other vehicles approaching from behind. All these types of lights play a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in agricultural operations.</p></p>
