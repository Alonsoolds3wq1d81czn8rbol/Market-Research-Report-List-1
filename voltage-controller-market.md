<p><p>Voltage controllers are essential components in regulating and maintaining the desired voltage levels in electrical systems. There are two main market types for voltage controllers: single phase and three phase. Single phase voltage controllers are typically used in residential and small commercial applications, while three phase voltage controllers are used in larger industrial settings. Single phase controllers manage voltage for single-phase AC circuits, while three phase controllers regulate voltage for three-phase AC circuits. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth functioning of electrical equipment and machinery.</p></p>
