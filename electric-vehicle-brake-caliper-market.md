<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Brake Caliper market consists of two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have one piston on each side of the brake rotor, providing basic braking performance. On the other hand, Multi-Piston Calipers have multiple pistons on each side of the rotor, offering higher braking power and improved control. Both types are essential components in electric vehicles to ensure safe and efficient braking systems.</p></p>
