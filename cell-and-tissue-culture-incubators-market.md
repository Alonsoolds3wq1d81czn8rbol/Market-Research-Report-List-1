<p><ul><li>Air-jacketed Incubators</li><li>Water-jacketed Incubators</li><li>Direct Heat Incubators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell and tissue culture incubators are commonly classified into three types: air-jacketed incubators, water-jacketed incubators, and direct heat incubators. Air-jacketed incubators use heated air to maintain a constant temperature, while water-jacketed incubators utilize water circulation to control temperature. Direct heat incubators directly heat the chamber to achieve desired temperature levels. Each type of incubator has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications depending on the specific requirements of cell and tissue culture experiments.</p></p>
