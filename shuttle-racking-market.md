<p><strong>The Shuttle Racking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Shuttle Racking</li><li>Sub Shuttle Racking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shuttle racking systems are automated storage solutions used in warehouses to improve efficiency and maximize storage capacity. Single shuttle racking involves a single shuttle carrying goods within the system, making it suitable for smaller warehouses with moderate storage needs. On the other hand, sub shuttle racking utilizes multiple shuttles operating independently within the system, allowing for greater flexibility and speed in handling goods. This makes sub shuttle racking ideal for larger warehouses with high-volume storage requirements. Both types of shuttle racking offer efficient and space-saving storage solutions for various warehouse operations.</p></p>
