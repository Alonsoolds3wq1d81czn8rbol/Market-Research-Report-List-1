<p><ul><li>Tubular Process</li><li>Autoclave Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE extrusion coating is a process used in packaging industries for applying a thin layer of LDPE onto various substrates. The two main types of LDPE extrusion coating processes are Tubular Process and Autoclave Process. The Tubular Process involves extruding LDPE through a circular die onto a moving substrate, while the Autoclave Process involves extruding LDPE into a closed chamber where it is coated onto a moving substrate. Both processes are widely used in the packaging industry for their efficiency and versatility in coating different materials.</p></p>
