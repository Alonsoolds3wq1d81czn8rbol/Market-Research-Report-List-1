<p><p>Polyethylene resins market can be classified into different types based on their density and molecular structure. The main types include Low Density Polyethylene Resins, High Density Polyethylene Resins, and Linear Low Density Polyethylene Resins. Low Density Polyethylene resins are flexible and have good impact strength, High Density Polyethylene resins are rigid and strong, while Linear Low Density Polyethylene resins offer a balance between flexibility and strength. Other types of polyethylene resins may include Medium Density Polyethylene and Ultra-high-molecular-weight Polyethylene among others.</p></p>
