<p><p>The Condenser Expander market includes various types of expanders, such as Conical Expander and Flared Pipe Expander. Conical expanders are designed with a tapered shape to gradually expand the material, while Flared Pipe Expanders are used to widen the end of pipes for connecting with other pipes or fittings. Both types serve different purposes in the condenser industry, with Conical Expanders being more suitable for precise expansion and Flared Pipe Expanders for creating secure connections between pipes.</p></p>
