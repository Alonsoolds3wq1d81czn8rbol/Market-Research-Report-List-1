<p><p>Poultry Insurance Market includes two types of insurance: Non-Commercial Mortality Insurance and Commercial Mortality Insurance. Non-Commercial Mortality Insurance provides coverage for individuals who own a small number of poultry, such as backyard farmers. This type of insurance helps protect against unexpected poultry losses. Commercial Mortality Insurance, on the other hand, is designed for commercial poultry farmers who have larger operations. It covers poultry losses due to various risks, such as disease outbreak, natural disasters, or accidents. These insurance types aim to mitigate financial risks associated with poultry farming.</p></p>
