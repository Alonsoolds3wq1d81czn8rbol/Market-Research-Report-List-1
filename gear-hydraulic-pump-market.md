<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>Gear Ring Pump</li><li>Screw Spindle Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gear Hydraulic Pump market consists of four main types: External Gear Pump, Internal Gear Pump, Gear Ring Pump, and Screw Spindle Pump. External Gear Pump utilizes two rotating gears to pump fluid, while Internal Gear Pump uses an inner gear that rotates inside an outer gear. Gear Ring Pump features a ring of gear teeth that operate together, and Screw Spindle Pump involves a helical screw spinning within a threaded cavity to move fluid. Each type has its unique design and functionality within the hydraulic pump industry.</p></p>
