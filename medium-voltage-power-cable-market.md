<p><p>Medium voltage power cables are available in three main types: copper, aluminum, and aluminum alloy. Copper cables are known for their high conductivity and durability, making them suitable for high-demand applications. Aluminum cables are lighter and more cost-effective than copper, making them a popular choice for medium voltage power transmission. Aluminum alloy cables combine the advantages of both copper and aluminum, offering a balance between conductivity, cost, and weight for medium voltage power distribution networks.</p></p>
