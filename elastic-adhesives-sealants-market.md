<p><p>Elastic adhesives and sealants are essential in various industries due to their flexibility and durability. The three main types in the market are polyurethane, silicone, and silyl-modified polymers (SMP). Polyurethane adhesives and sealants provide excellent adhesion and resist harsh environmental conditions. Silicone adhesives offer resistance to high temperatures and flexibility. SMP products combine the benefits of both polyurethane and silicone, offering high adhesion strength and weather resistance. Each type caters to different applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
