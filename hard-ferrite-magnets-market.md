<p><ul><li>Sintered Ferrite Magnet</li><li>Bonded Ferrite Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hard ferrite magnets are commonly categorized into two main types: sintered ferrite magnets and bonded ferrite magnets. Sintered ferrite magnets are produced through the process of powder metallurgy, resulting in high magnetic properties and excellent resistance to demagnetization. On the other hand, bonded ferrite magnets are made by mixing ferrite powder with a polymer binder, offering flexibility in shape and size but with slightly lower magnetic properties. Both types of hard ferrite magnets are widely used in various industries for their cost-effectiveness and durability.</p></p>
