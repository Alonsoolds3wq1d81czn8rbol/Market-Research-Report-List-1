In the CMC market, SiC fibres are incorporated into a ceramic matrix to improve strength, heat resistance, and chemical stability. In the MMC market, SiC fibres are utilized in a metal matrix to enhance strength, stiffness, and wear resistance. These applications highlight the versatility and potential of SiC fibres in various composite materials.</p></p>
