<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber Textile Reinforced Concrete</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber Textile Reinforced Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber Textile Reinforced Concrete refers to concrete that is reinforced with small, fine carbon fibers woven into a textile. This type of concrete is commonly used in applications where high strength and durability are required, such as bridges and buildings. </p><p>On the other hand, Large-Tow Carbon Fiber Textile Reinforced Concrete uses larger carbon fibers in the textile reinforcement, providing even greater strength and durability. This type of concrete is often used in heavy-duty construction projects where maximum resilience is essential, such as in tunnels or industrial structures.</p></p>
