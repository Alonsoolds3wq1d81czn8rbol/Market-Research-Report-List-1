<p><p>Biological mass spectrometry market includes various types such as Electrospray Ionization (ESI) and Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption Ionization (MALDI). ESI involves the creation of ions from a liquid sample, while MALDI uses a laser pulse to ionize molecules on a solid sample surface. Other markets in this field may include secondary ion mass spectrometry (SIMS) and time-of-flight (TOF) mass spectrometry. These techniques offer unique advantages for analyzing biological samples, such as high sensitivity and the ability to analyze complex mixtures.</p></p>
