<p><p>Light-duty starters are mainly categorized into two types: AC type and DC type. AC type light-duty starters are designed to start engines using alternating current, while DC type light-duty starters use direct current. Both types of starters are commonly used in smaller engines such as motorcycles, ATVs, and lawnmowers. AC type starters are typically more efficient and cost-effective, while DC type starters provide more precise speed control. Manufacturers and consumers have a choice between the two types based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
