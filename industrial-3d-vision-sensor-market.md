<p><p>Industrial 3D Vision Sensor market types include Photogrammetry Technology, which uses photos to measure distances and dimensions; Raster Structured Light, which projects a pattern onto an object and analyzes the deformation to create a 3D model; Digital Image Correlation, which compares images before and after deformation to calculate 3D information; and other technologies like Time-of-Flight and Stereoscopic Vision. These technologies offer a range of options for manufacturers looking to implement 3D vision systems in their industrial processes.</p></p>
