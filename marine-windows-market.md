<p><p>The marine windows market is categorized into three main types based on their design and functionality - Fixed Windows, Sliding Windows, and Hinged Windows. Fixed windows are stationary and do not open, providing a strong and secure barrier against the elements. Sliding windows can be opened horizontally to allow for ventilation while maintaining a streamlined appearance. Hinged windows open vertically or horizontally on a pivot, offering flexibility in terms of airflow and accessibility. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the marine industry.</p></p>
