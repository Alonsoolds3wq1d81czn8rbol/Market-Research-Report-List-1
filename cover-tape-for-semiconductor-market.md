<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Type</li><li>Pressure Sensitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cover tapes for semiconductors come in two main types: heat activated and pressure sensitive. Heat activated cover tape requires a specific temperature to be applied in order to adhere to the semiconductor, providing a secure seal. On the other hand, pressure sensitive cover tape adheres to the semiconductor simply by applying pressure, making it a quicker and easier option. Both types offer protection and containment for semiconductors during storage and transportation, ensuring their integrity and efficiency.</p></p>
