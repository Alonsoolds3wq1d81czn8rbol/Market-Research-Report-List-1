<p><p>Power semiconductor discrete devices market is segmented into various types, including diodes, IGBTs, MOSFETs, bipolar transistors (BJTs), and thyristors. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, while IGBTs combine the high input impedance of a MOSFET with the high-current capability of a bipolar transistor. MOSFETs are voltage-controlled transistors, BJT allows current amplification, and thyristors are used for high-power applications. Each type caters to different power requirements and specific applications in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
