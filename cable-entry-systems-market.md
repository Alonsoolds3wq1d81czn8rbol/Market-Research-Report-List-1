<p><p>Cable entry systems for cables without connectors refer to solutions that allow cables to enter enclosures or equipment without connectors attached. These systems provide sealing to ensure protection against environmental factors such as dust and moisture. On the other hand, cable entry systems for cables with connectors are designed to accommodate cables that already have connectors attached. These systems typically feature customizable entry points to accommodate various connector sizes and types, while still providing sealing and protection for the cable entry point.</p></p>
