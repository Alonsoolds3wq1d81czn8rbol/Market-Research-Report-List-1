<p><ul><li>Analog PIR Sensors</li><li>Digital PIR Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PIR (Passive Infrared) sensors are classified into two main types based on their output signal processing methods. Analog PIR sensors produce a continuous output signal proportional to the detected infrared radiation, while digital PIR sensors convert the analog signal into a digital format for easier processing and interpretation. Analog sensors are typically used in simpler applications where precise detection is not critical, while digital sensors are more commonly used in advanced security systems and automation applications requiring accurate and reliable detection capabilities.</p></p>
