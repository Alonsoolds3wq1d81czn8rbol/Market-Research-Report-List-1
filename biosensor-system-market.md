<p><p>Biosensor systems are used in various industries for detecting and measuring specific biological or chemical analytes. There are three main types of biosensor systems in the market - Thermal, Electrochemical, and Optical. Thermal biosensors measure changes in temperature caused by a biological reaction. Electrochemical biosensors detect analytes through electrical signals. Optical biosensors use light to detect and measure analytes. Each type has its own unique advantages and is used in different applications depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
